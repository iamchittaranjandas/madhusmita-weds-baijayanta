Sacred Odia Rituals

Mangarula Krutya ମଙ୍ଗଳକୃତ୍ୟ

Auspicious morning prayers and painting of the traditional rice-paste *Chita/Alpana* floor drawings to invoke blessings.

Hasta Ganthi ହସ୍ତଗଣ୍ଠି

Tying of the sacred nuptial knot over the bride and groom's hands, establishing their lifetime commitment.

Sata Ghera ସାତଘେର

The couple circles the sacred Homa fire seven times (Saptapadi), taking solemn vows for their marital journey.

Kanya Bida କନ୍ୟା ବିଦା

The emotional departure of the bride as she leaves her home, traditionally carried in a decorated *Doli* carriage.
